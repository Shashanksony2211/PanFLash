Our Story

We are a sound systems manufacturing unit with a crucial aspect of the manufacturing industry, providing customized products for different sectors. Pankaj Kumar Sharma is the founder of this company. He worked as country manager for Forme mobile company (China), similar experience in Mobile Battery manufacturing and Cellio. After these job stints, he started his journey of manufacturing and parts in 2009.

We were involved in manufacturing of Mobile Accessories like Mobile Charger, data cable, cables, ear-phones, and Bluetooth, full range speakers like of fans, PA series audio equipment, small trolley setups, table lamps etc.

During the manufacturing journey we had many manufacturing sectors like PCB Unit, Li-Battery pack and backup systems manufacturing, Wire manufacturing Unit, Home Electronics Unit, Injection moulding unit and have Printing inside of PCB machine for direct automation and day tagging manufacturing etc.
